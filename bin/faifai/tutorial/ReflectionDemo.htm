Colour Reflection Demo

Learning objectives: to describe the colour reflected off a 'white' plane when different colours of light is shining upon it.

A plane with a white surface means that the plane looks white when it is shone upon by white light. A white surface does not absorb any colour of light.

The white plane is near the bottom of the screen

The point light source is to the top hand side of the plane

Change the colour of the light source and the light reflected off the plane is the same as the light source
